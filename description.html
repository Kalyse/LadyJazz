About Lazy Jazz

This is an exceptional example of a well cared for vessel, with beautifully designed interior and recently refurbished leather.


// About Lazy Jazz

The beautifully designed, spacious saloon, galley and cabins give the feeling normally associated with far bigger boats.

The boat recently (March 2012) had an extensive refit consisting of re-upholstering the saloon, lower helm and dinning areas in top quality Ultra leather and the entire boat was re carpeted throughout. The saloon also benefits from an “overcarpet”, thereby ensuring that the fitted carpet remains as good as new.

Huge saloon, dinning area and large en-suite forward and aft cabins give the feeling you are on board a much larger vessel than Lady Jazz actually is.

Both of the Volvo TAMD63P (approximately 600 hours) engines were serviced in March 2012 by a leading Volvo approved dealership and since the service have worked for approximately 80 hours.

The master aft cabin has its own separate shower compartment along with its large master island bed and large mirrored vanity unit with drawers.

The dinette & Saloon also both convert to another 2 double beds giving a total of 4 double beds, sleeping 8 guests in comfort The large Saloon seats 8 guests and large dinette seats 4, Internally entertaining 12 guests in comfort. 

The external cockpit & fly-bridge seating also accommodates these 12 guests & more. Style, comfort, luxury and performance exude from this beautiful boat and this must be the best value F43 on the market today. From the moment you set foot on the real teak decking you know you are looking at a great example of arguably Sealine's best model.

The high level of specification is backed up by high level of documentation and every receipt, user manual and service document has been retained.
